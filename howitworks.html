How It Works

Getting fresh, affordable produce is simple. Here's how Quirky Harvest brings the farm to your door.

1. Sign Up

Create your account and tell us your Maryland county. This helps us connect you with local farms nearby.

2. Get Matched

We pair you with nearby farmers and hand-select a box of quirky, healthy produce based on availability and season.

3. Eat & Enjoy

Your produce is delivered weekly. Enjoy cooking and eating healthy food while reducing waste and supporting your community!
