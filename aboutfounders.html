Our Mission

A community-powered food distribution platform connecting Maryland families to fresh, local farm produce at affordable prices.

Our Purpose & Vision

Our purpose is to bridge the gap between local farms and underserved communities by offering an affordable, tech-driven delivery solution for healthy food.

Our vision is to revolutionize food access by becoming the go-to for families of all backgrounds by providing a subscription service across the U.S. beginning in Maryland.
